<p><p>The Friction Products and Materials market encompasses two primary types: dry and wet friction materials. Dry friction materials, such as brake pads and clutches, operate without lubrication, relying on surface texture and material composition to generate friction. Conversely, wet friction materials are used in applications like hydraulic brakes and transmissions, where lubricants are present. These materials are designed to maintain consistent performance under varying conditions, enhancing efficiency and durability in various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
