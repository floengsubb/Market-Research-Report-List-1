<p><p>The laser level market comprises various types, including dot laser levels, line laser levels, rotary laser levels, and torpedo laser levels. Dot laser levels project a single dot for precise point alignment. Line laser levels emit horizontal or vertical beams for straight-line applications. Rotary laser levels create 360-degree beams for extensive coverage, ideal for large areas. Torpedo laser levels are compact and portable, designed for tight spaces, providing accurate leveling solutions in versatile work environments. Each type serves distinct user needs in construction and surveying.</p></p>
