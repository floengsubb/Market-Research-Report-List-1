<p><p>The pickup camper market is primarily divided into two main types: hard side pickup campers and pop-up pickup campers. Hard side campers feature rigid walls and provide enhanced insulation and durability, making them suitable for year-round use and rough terrain. In contrast, pop-up campers are lightweight with collapsible sides, allowing for easier towing and storage. They offer a more compact option but may require setup time. Both types cater to different preferences for mobility, comfort, and adventure among outdoor enthusiasts.</p></p>
