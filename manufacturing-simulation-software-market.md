<p><p>Manufacturing simulation software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based manufacturing simulation software offers flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access simulations via the internet, ensuring easier updates and collaboration across different locations. Conversely, on-premise manufacturing simulation software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization options but often requiring more upfront investment and maintenance. Both types cater to specific needs within the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
