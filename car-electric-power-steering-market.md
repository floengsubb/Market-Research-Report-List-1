<p><strong>The Car Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Electric Power Steering (EPS) market is categorized into three main types: C-EPS, P-EPS, and R-EPS. C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering) uses an electric motor integrated into the steering column for precise control. P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) applies electric assistance directly to the pinion gear, enhancing responsiveness. R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) features electric motors attached to the steering rack, providing a more efficient and lighter design. Each type offers benefits in efficiency, weight reduction, and driving experience.</p></p>
