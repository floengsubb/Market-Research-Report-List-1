<p><strong>The Smart Syringe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Auto-disable syringes</li><li>Active safety syringes</li><li>Passive safety syringes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart syringe market comprises three main types: auto-disable syringes, active safety syringes, and passive safety syringes. Auto-disable syringes automatically lock after a single use, preventing reuse. Active safety syringes require the user to activate a safety mechanism after injection to prevent needle stick injuries. In contrast, passive safety syringes feature built-in safety mechanisms that engage automatically after use, minimizing the risk of accidental needle exposure. Each type enhances safety and infection control in medical settings.</p></p>
