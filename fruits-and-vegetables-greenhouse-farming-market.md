<p><p>Fruits and vegetables greenhouse farming primarily utilizes two main market types: glass and plastic greenhouses. Glass greenhouses offer superior light transmission, durability, and long-term use, making them ideal for high-value crops. However, they come with higher initial costs. In contrast, plastic greenhouses, often made of polyethylene, are more affordable and easier to install, providing good insulation and light diffusion. While they have a shorter lifespan, their lightweight, flexible design allows for quick adaptation to various growing conditions, making them popular among small-scale farmers.</p></p>
