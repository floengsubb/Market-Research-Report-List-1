<p><p>The plant-based cheese market includes various types catering to diverse dietary preferences. Soy cheese is made from fermented soybeans, offering a creamy texture and protein content. Almond cheese utilizes almond milk, providing a nutty flavor and a lower calorie count. Cashew cheese is crafted from blended cashews, resulting in a rich, buttery taste ideal for spreading. Rice milk cheese is derived from rice, catering to those with nut allergies. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses diverse options like coconut and hemp-based cheeses, expanding consumer choices.</p></p>
