<p><ul><li>HER2 Inhibitors</li><li>Mitotic Inhibitors</li><li>Anti-Metabolites</li><li>Aromatase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breast cancer treatment drugs market includes various types of therapies targeting specific cancer characteristics. HER2 inhibitors are designed to block the human epidermal growth factor receptor 2, effective in HER2-positive cancers. Mitotic inhibitors disrupt the cell division process, preventing cancer cell proliferation. Anti-metabolites interfere with DNA synthesis, targeting rapidly dividing cancer cells. Aromatase inhibitors reduce estrogen levels, beneficial for hormone receptor-positive cancers. Each category plays a crucial role in personalized treatment strategies, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
