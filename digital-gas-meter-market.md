<p><strong>The Digital Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital gas meter market encompasses two main types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR systems enable utility companies to collect meter readings remotely via handheld or mobile devices, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. In contrast, AMI offers a more comprehensive approach, integrating two-way communication between the meter and utility infrastructure. This allows for real-time data monitoring, demand response capabilities, and improved customer service. Both technologies contribute to smarter energy management and operational cost savings.</p></p>
