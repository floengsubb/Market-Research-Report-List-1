<p><strong>The Pulp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"BSK"</li><li>"BHK"</li><li>"HYP"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulp market is categorized into three main types: BSK, BHK, and HYP. BSK, or bleached softwood kraft pulp, is primarily used for high-strength papers and board products, known for its durability. BHK, or bleached hardwood kraft pulp, is derived from hardwoods and is favored for its brightness and softness, making it ideal for printing and writing papers. HYP, or high-yield pulp, is produced through mechanical processing and is often used in newsprint and lower-grade papers, maximizing fiber utilization.</p></p>
