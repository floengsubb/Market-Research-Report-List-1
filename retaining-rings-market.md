<p><ul><li>External Retaining Ring</li><li>Internal Retaining Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retaining rings market is categorized into two primary types: external retaining rings and internal retaining rings. External retaining rings are designed to fit over the shaft or outside of a bore, effectively holding components in place by utilizing a groove. In contrast, internal retaining rings fit inside a bore and secure elements by creating a barrier from within. These rings are essential in various industries for maintaining the position of components in assemblies, ensuring stability and reliability in mechanical systems.</p></p>
