<p><p>The screw expander market comprises two primary types: steam screw expanders and organic working medium screw expanders. Steam screw expanders utilize steam as a working fluid, converting thermal energy into mechanical work, often in power generation systems. Organic working medium screw expanders operate with organic fluids, typically in organic Rankine cycles, to harness waste heat or improve energy efficiency in industrial processes. Both technologies enhance energy recovery and efficiency, catering to diverse applications in renewable energy and industrial sectors.</p></p>
