<p><p>The UV curing coatings market encompasses several key components: monomers, oligomers, photoinitiators, and polyurethane (PU) dispersions. Monomers are low molecular weight compounds that react during the curing process, enhancing the final product's properties. Oligomers are medium-sized molecules that provide structure and performance benefits, while photoinitiators trigger the curing reaction when exposed to UV light. PU dispersions combine the properties of polyurethane with other components, offering flexibility and durability in coatings, making them suitable for various applications across industries.</p></p>
