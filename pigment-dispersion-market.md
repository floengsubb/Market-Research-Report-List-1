<p><p>The pigment dispersion market is categorized into three main types: inorganic pigments, organic pigments, and others. Inorganic pigments, derived from mineral sources, provide stability and durability, making them ideal for applications in construction and automotive coatings. Organic pigments, synthesized from carbon-containing compounds, boast vibrant colors and excellent tinting strength, typically used in plastics and textiles. The "others" category encompasses specialty pigments and those with unique properties, catering to niche applications such as cosmetics and foodstuffs.</p></p>
