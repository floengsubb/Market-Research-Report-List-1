<p><p>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) resin market comprises two primary types: Tubular EVA and Autoclave EVA. Tubular EVA is produced through a continuous tubular process, resulting in high clarity, low gel content, and suitability for applications like packaging and films. Conversely, Autoclave EVA is made in batch processes under controlled conditions, offering excellent mechanical properties and flexibility, making it ideal for applications in adhesives, footwear, and automotive sectors. Both types cater to diverse industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
