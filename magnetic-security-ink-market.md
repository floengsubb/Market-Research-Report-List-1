<p><ul><li>Ferrite Magnetic Ink</li><li>Rare Earth Magnetic Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic security ink market is primarily divided into two types: ferrite magnetic ink and rare earth magnetic ink. Ferrite magnetic ink contains iron oxide particles, providing good magnetic properties at a lower cost, making it suitable for applications like banknotes and security documents. In contrast, rare earth magnetic ink utilizes advanced materials like neodymium for enhanced magnetic characteristics, offering greater performance and durability, ideal for high-security applications. Both types enhance document authentication and fraud prevention through their unique magnetic properties.</p></p>
