<p><strong>The Agricultural Fogging Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Agricultural Fogging Machines</li><li>Cold Agricultural Fogging Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural fogging machines market features two primary types: thermal and cold fogging machines. Thermal agricultural fogging machines utilize heat to vaporize pesticide solutions, creating a fine mist that effectively penetrates crops and weeds. They are ideal for larger areas and provide long-lasting residual effects. In contrast, cold agricultural fogging machines operate at lower temperatures, producing smaller droplets that disperse quickly and are suitable for targeted applications, including indoor spaces. Both types enhance pest control and crop protection in agriculture.</p></p>
