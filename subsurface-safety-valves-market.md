<p><ul><li>Ground Control Safety Valves</li><li>Subsurface Control Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsurface safety valve market is divided into two main types: Ground Control Safety Valves and Subsurface Control Safety Valves. Ground Control Safety Valves are designed for surface operations, providing an automated method to control well flow and ensure safety during drilling or production. In contrast, Subsurface Control Safety Valves are installed downhole and are crucial for preventing uncontrolled fluid flow during emergencies, protecting both equipment and personnel. Both types are essential for maintaining safety and operational efficiency in oil and gas exploration.</p></p>
